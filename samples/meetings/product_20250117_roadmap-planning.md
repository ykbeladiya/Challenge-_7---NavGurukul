# Product Roadmap Planning - January 17, 2025

**Attendees**: Ivy (Product Manager), Jack (Engineer), Kelly (Designer), Liam (Stakeholder)

## Objectives

1. Plan Q2 2025 roadmap
2. Align on feature priorities
3. Establish planning process

## Roadmap Planning Process

Ivy: "Let's establish our roadmap planning workflow:

1. **Gather stakeholder requirements**: Conduct interviews and surveys with key stakeholders
2. **Prioritize features**: Use RICE framework to score and rank features
3. **Create roadmap timeline**: Map features to quarters and assign resources"

Jack: "What's the RICE framework?"

Ivy: "RICE stands for Reach, Impact, Confidence, and Effort. It's a prioritization scoring method that provides objective scoring and aligns stakeholders on priorities."

## Definitions

**Roadmap**: A strategic plan outlining product features and timeline.

**RICE Framework**: Reach, Impact, Confidence, Effort - a prioritization scoring method.

## FAQs

**Q: How often should we update the roadmap?**  
A: Quarterly reviews with monthly adjustments based on feedback.

**Q: Who approves the roadmap?**  
A: Product leadership and key stakeholders must approve before publication.

## Decision

We will adopt the RICE framework for feature prioritization. This provides objective scoring and aligns stakeholders on priorities.
